Neighborhood Restaurant and Bars
This app will display given restaurant and bar locations on a google map
and list those same locations in a side-bar menu. Simply click on the name
of the location in the list, or click on its map marker, and you will see
information about that location as provided by Yelp.

Filtering
To filter down the list and corresponding map markers, type the name of
the location in the search bar. You can also filter by keyword, such as
"pizza" or "american". The page will update in realtime as you type.

Instructions
Download all files from the repository to your local machine
Launch the app by opening index.html
